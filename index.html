Execution

we decided to do something adventurous, as we live in plains to decided to go uphill,
towards the mountain, after a lot of discussion, debates we decided to go for rafting,
we started searching for nearby rafting spot, after a lot of research we got to know about "RISHIKESH" a religious place. It is located around 330 km .
we put our destination on google maps and started our road trip. It was a roughly 8 hours ride taking halts at dhabas and restaurants. we reached Rishikesh around 5 pm. we decided to book a budget-friendly hotel. as we were late for rafting and tired of driving we planned to do rafting the next day. we decided to see famous tourist places like Lakshman jhula a well-known bridge on the river Ganga which is famous all around the globe. it is a marvelous bridge. and look awesome at night with wonderful lighting. we decided to camp on the bank of river Ganga. it was a wonderful night. weather was great with a cold breeze and the sound of the river flowing, moving leaves was like the cherry on the cake.

Rafting

Finally the day arrived we were super excited for rafting .our excitement vanished all our tiredness , we booked a ride from rafting point .there were 4 more people with us . we gathered at marine drive which is 24 km away from rishikesh . we were provied with safety jackes as safety is utmost importants we were not allowed to take electronical items with us as there was chances of damage to the electrical items . we wlso submitted our expensive items . there was a training of approx 15 before rafting , in which we were taught how to use raft paddles . how to cntrol the speed of rafting boat . what should we do in case of emergency etc and etc.
now we were all ready for rafting . i was little nervous after seeing thwe waves but excited at the same time . It was a thrilling experenice ,at some place river was quiet at some place it was aggresive , at some place i was about to fall but survived :) it was around 5 hour stretch from marine drive to rishikesh covering a distance of 24 km falling in grade 2 and grade 3. it was extremely thrilling expereince.which should be experienced by each and every person once in a lifetime . With today technology it bacame safe and more thrilling.

Detail about routes

When you reach Rishikesh you can find multiple organisation who are in the buisness of rafting where you can book offline or you can book online by visiting their websites. The fare of rafting is based on how much distance you want to travel the shorter the distance less is the fare and increase in the price of fare by increase of distance. the price vary from organisations to organisation.

Distance(in km) Time starting point Grade fare(inr)
9 1 Hour brahmpuri Grade I-II 600
16 2 hours Shivpuri Grade II-III 1000
26 3.5 Hours Marine Drive Grade II-III 1500
35 5-6 Hours Kaudiyala Grade II-IV 2500

Many people “aspire” to run Grade V, but in all honesty, Grade V and Grade III can be just as much fun. There is no need for previous experience to run any grade of river or rapid, as your guide team have run these rapids many times and will make expert judgements both on and off the river. You will be one member of an excellent paddling team that allows you to challenge big whitewater safely, having faith not only in your guide, but in your team of fellow adventurers too. There are a variety of grades of rapid on each river, so even though there are some Grade V rapids on the Zambezi, there are also Grade II-IV rapids as well.
