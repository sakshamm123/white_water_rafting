How It Started

It all started when me and my friends
were playing BGMI and talking about past
, future as we have recently cleared +2
but sadly from the online class, we created
memories we do not
meet very often. As the virus was
disappearing and new normal was
replacing by a new normal or you can
say old normal so we decided to plan a
trip which can be remembered forever
before everyone creates a new normal

Execution

we decided to do something adventurous,
as we live in plains to decided to go uphill,
towards the mountain, after a lot of
discussion, debates we decided to go
for rafting,we started searching for nearby rafting
spot, after a lot of research we got to know about
"RISHIKESH" a religious place. It is located around
330 km .
.
we put our destination on google maps and started
our road trip. It was a roughly 8 hours ride taking
halts at dhabas and restaurants. we reached Rishikesh around 5 pm. we decided to book a budget-friendly hotel. as we were late for rafting and tired of driving we planned to do rafting the next day. we decided to see famous tourist places like Lakshman jhula a well-known bridge on the river Ganga which is famous all around the globe. it is a marvelous bridge. and look awesome at night with wonderful lighting. we decided to camp on the bank of river Ganga. it was a wonderful night. weather was great with a cold breeze and the sound of the river flowing, moving leaves was like the cherry on the cake.

Rafting

Finally the day arrived we were super excited for rafting .our excitement vanish all our tiredness
